Our Story

Raised in the
Coulee Country

Steve still farms the same land his father and grandfather did — land that raised his children and is now cherished by his grandchildren. A member of the American Wagyu Association, he has built a herd of full-blooded Wagyu cattle at least 98% pure, every animal with registered genetics proving its pedigree. We are dedicated to raising the finest quality Wagyu in a sustainable way — and being stewards of the land that has been in this family for generations.

Our cattle roam freely across our La Crosse farm — hormone-free and antibiotic-free, always. American Wagyu bred from world-class genetics, delivering the signature buttery marbling that sets Miller Wagyu apart from anything at a grocery store.
