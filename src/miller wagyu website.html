Our Story

Raised in the
Coulee Country

Steve and Sally Miller have farmed all their lives — both raised on dairy farms in the beautiful coulee region of La Crosse. Farming isn't just what they do; it's who they are. That tradition of care, quality, and hard work is baked into every animal they raise.

"We only know one way to farm — and that's raising quality beef the right way. Something we've passed down to our children and grandchildren."

— Steve & Sally Miller, Founders

Our Promise

What Makes Miller Wagyu Different

🌾
Free Range & Pasture Raised

Our cattle roam freely across our La Crosse farm, living the way cattle should — with space, fresh air, and natural forage.

🚫
No Hormones. No Antibiotics.

Never. We believe clean beef is better beef, and we stand behind every cut with that commitment to natural raising.

🔬
Internationally Researched Genetics

Our American Wagyu cattle are bred from carefully selected genetics, delivering that signature marbling and buttery flavor.

📦
Processing Included

Every wholesale order includes custom processing — cut to your specs, vacuum-sealed, and ready for your freezer.
