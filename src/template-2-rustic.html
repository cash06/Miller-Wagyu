Our Story

Raised in the
Coulee Country

Steve and Sally Miller have farmed all their lives — both raised on dairy farms in the beautiful coulee region of La Crosse. Farming isn't just what they do; it's who they are. That tradition of care, quality, and hard work is baked into every animal they raise.

"We only know one way to farm — and that's raising quality beef the right way. Something we've passed down to our children and grandchildren."

— Steve & Sally Miller, Founders
