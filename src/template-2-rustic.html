Our Story

Raised in the
Coulee Country

Steve still farms the same land his father and grandfather did — land that raised his children and is now cherished by his grandchildren. A member of the American Wagyu Association, Steve has built a herd of full-blooded Wagyu cattle that are at least 98% pure, every one with registered genetics proving its pedigree.

The Miller Method

How we raise
beef differently

01
The Genetics

Steve is a member of the American Wagyu Association. Every animal in the herd is at least 98% pure Wagyu with registered genetics proving its pedigree — so you know exactly what you're getting. There are no regulations on Wagyu labeling; registered genetics are the only real proof.

AWA Member · 98% Pure
02
The Land

Steve farms the same coulee-country land his father and grandfather farmed. Not a contract operation, not a venture farm — family land in the beautiful Coulee Region of Wisconsin that his grandchildren now cherish.

La Crosse, Wisconsin
03
The Commitment

Zero hormones. Zero antibiotics. No exceptions, no asterisks. We've never used them and we never will. Clean beef is better beef — and the higher monounsaturated fat content in Wagyu makes it genuinely healthier than conventional beef.

Always. No Exceptions.
04
Know Your Farmer

You order from us directly. We cut it to your specs, vacuum-seal it, and it comes to you frozen. No middlemen, no grocery markup, no mystery. And if you ever want to see where your food comes from — stop by the farm.
