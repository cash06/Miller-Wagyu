Our Story

Raised in the
Coulee Country

Steve still farms the same land his father and grandfather did — land that raised his children and is now cherished by his grandchildren. A member of the American Wagyu Association, Steve has spent years building a herd of full-blooded Wagyu cattle that are all at least 98% pure Wagyu, every one with registered genetics proving its pedigree.

The Miller Standard

Why our
Wagyu is
different.

01
Same land. Three generations.

Steve farms the same coulee-country land his father and grandfather did. This isn't a venture farm or a contract operation — it's family land that his children were raised on and his grandchildren love.

02
At least 98% pure Wagyu. Every animal. Registered.

Steve is a member of the American Wagyu Association. Every animal in the herd has registered genetics proving its pedigree — so you know exactly what you're eating. No guessing, no marketing claims.

03
Zero hormones. Zero antibiotics. No exceptions.

Not "raised without added hormones." Not "antibiotic-free when possible." Never. That's a promise we've kept since the first day and we're not stopping.

04
Know your farmer. Buy direct.

There are no regulations on Wagyu labeling — anyone can call their beef Wagyu. We back ours with registered genetics and an open invitation: stop by the farm and see for yourself.
